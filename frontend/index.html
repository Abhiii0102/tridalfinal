🌐 About Tridalworld

Tridalworld is a professionally managed, multi-category enterprise engaged in the import, wholesale distribution, and retail of electrical, electronic, lifestyle, household, and pure traditional kitchen products.

Along with product trading, the company offers advanced Fabric Design, CAD Drafting, Automation Design, and Architectural Design Support, making it a versatile and future-ready organization.

Tridalworld is proudly **GST-Registered & MSME-Certified**, ensuring compliance, reliability, and authenticity in every business process.
